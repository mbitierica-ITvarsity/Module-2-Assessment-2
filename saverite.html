About

Where everyone is welcome, Saverite Supermarkets is a franchise supermarket with over 145 stores.

Saverite started in 2008 and has since grown in both South Africa and in neighbouring countries.

Saverite Supermarkets leverage off the muscle of Masscash to ensure competitive pricing for the franchise and uses the Masscash footprint of over 70 wholesalers.
